ORDER RISK RESOURCE
===================

DESCRIPTION:
The Order Risk resource represents fraud risk assessments for orders. Risks indicate potential fraudulent activity and help merchants make informed decisions about whether to fulfill orders.

PURPOSE:
- Assess fraud risk
- Flag suspicious orders
- Aid fulfillment decisions
- Integrate fraud detection

KEY FIELDS:
- id: Unique identifier
- order_id: Associated order
- checkout_id: Associated checkout
- source: Risk source/provider
- score: Risk score (0.0 to 1.0)
- recommendation: accept, investigate, cancel
- display: Show to merchant (true/false)
- cause_cancel: Should trigger cancellation
- message: Explanation of the risk
- merchant_message: Merchant-facing message

RISK SCORES:
- 0.0 - 0.3: Low risk (typically accept)
- 0.3 - 0.7: Medium risk (investigate)
- 0.7 - 1.0: High risk (consider cancelling)

RECOMMENDATIONS:
- accept: Order appears safe
- investigate: Review before fulfilling
- cancel: High fraud probability

RISK SOURCES:
- shopify: Shopify's fraud analysis
- external: Third-party fraud services
- custom: App-created risks

AVAILABLE OPERATIONS:
- Find: Get a specific risk assessment
- List All: Get all risks for an order
- Create: Add a new risk assessment
- Update: Modify risk details
- Delete: Remove a risk assessment

USE CASES:
1. Fraud Prevention: Identify risky orders
2. Manual Review: Flag for investigation
3. Automated Rules: Auto-cancel high-risk
4. Fraud Apps: Add custom risk assessments
5. Order Processing: Inform fulfillment decisions
