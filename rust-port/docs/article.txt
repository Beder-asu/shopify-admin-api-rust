ARTICLE RESOURCE
================

DESCRIPTION:
The Article resource represents a blog post within a Shopify store's blog. Articles are used to publish content, share news, provide tutorials, and engage with customers through a store's blog section.

PURPOSE:
- Publish blog content on the store
- Support content marketing strategies
- SEO optimization through blog posts
- Customer engagement and education

KEY FIELDS:
- id: Unique identifier for the article
- title: Article title
- blog_id: ID of the blog this article belongs to
- author: Author name
- user_id: ID of the Shopify user who created the article
- body_html: Main content of the article (HTML)
- summary_html: Article summary/excerpt (HTML)
- handle: URL-friendly slug
- tags: Comma-separated tags for categorization
- image: Featured image for the article
- created_at: When the article was created
- published_at: When the article was published
- updated_at: When the article was last updated
- template_suffix: Custom template suffix

AVAILABLE OPERATIONS:
- Find: Get a specific article from a blog
- List All: Get all articles from a blog
- Count: Count articles in a blog
- Create: Create a new article
- Update: Update an existing article
- Delete: Remove an article
- Authors: Get list of article authors
- Tags: Get list of article tags

USE CASES:
1. Content Management: Create and manage blog posts programmatically
2. Content Migration: Import articles from external systems
3. Auto-Publishing: Schedule and publish content automatically
4. SEO Tools: Manage meta information and tags
5. Content Syndication: Sync content across platforms
