CUSTOMER RESOURCE
=================

DESCRIPTION:
The Customer resource represents a customer account in the Shopify store. It contains personal information, order history references, addresses, and marketing preferences. Customers can be created through purchases or directly via the API.

PURPOSE:
- Manage customer accounts
- Track customer information and preferences
- Enable personalized experiences
- Support CRM integrations

KEY FIELDS:
- id: Unique identifier for the customer
- email: Customer email address
- first_name / last_name: Customer name
- phone: Phone number
- accepts_marketing: Email marketing consent
- accepts_marketing_updated_at: When consent was updated
- marketing_opt_in_level: Level of marketing consent
- addresses: Array of saved addresses
- default_address: Primary address
- orders_count: Total number of orders
- total_spent: Lifetime spend amount
- last_order_id / last_order_name: Most recent order
- note: Staff notes about the customer
- tags: Comma-separated tags
- state: Account state (disabled, invited, enabled, declined)
- verified_email: Whether email is verified
- tax_exempt: Tax exemption status
- tax_exemptions: Applied tax exemptions
- sms_marketing_consent: SMS marketing preferences

AVAILABLE OPERATIONS:
- Find: Get a customer by ID
- List All: Get customers with filters
- Search: Search customers by query
- Count: Count customers
- Create: Create a new customer
- Update: Modify customer details
- Delete: Remove a customer
- Send Invite: Send account activation email
- Account Activation URL: Get activation link

USE CASES:
1. CRM Integration: Sync with external CRM systems
2. Marketing: Segment customers for campaigns
3. Personalization: Customize experiences
4. Analytics: Analyze customer behavior
5. Support: Access customer history
