VARIANT RESOURCE
================

DESCRIPTION:
The Variant resource represents a specific version of a product with unique attributes like size or color. Each product can have multiple variants, each with its own price, SKU, inventory, and options.

PURPOSE:
- Define product variations
- Manage variant-specific data
- Track inventory per variant
- Handle variant pricing

KEY FIELDS:
- id: Unique identifier
- product_id: Parent product
- title: Variant title (auto-generated)
- price: Selling price
- compare_at_price: Original price
- sku: Stock keeping unit
- barcode: UPC/EAN/ISBN
- grams: Weight in grams
- weight: Weight value
- weight_unit: g, kg, lb, oz
- inventory_item_id: Inventory tracking
- inventory_quantity: Stock count
- old_inventory_quantity: Previous stock
- inventory_management: shopify or null
- inventory_policy: deny or continue
- fulfillment_service: manual or service
- requires_shipping: Physical product
- taxable: Subject to tax
- tax_code: Tax category (Plus)
- option1/option2/option3: Option values
- position: Display order
- image_id: Associated image
- presentment_prices: Multi-currency
- created_at / updated_at: Timestamps
- admin_graphql_api_id: GraphQL identifier

INVENTORY POLICY:
- deny: Stop selling when out of stock
- continue: Allow overselling

AVAILABLE OPERATIONS:
- Find: Get a specific variant
- List All: Get variants for product
- Count: Count variants
- Create: Add variant to product
- Update: Modify variant details
- Delete: Remove a variant

LIMITS:
- Maximum 100 variants per product
- Up to 3 options (size, color, material)

USE CASES:
1. Size/Color Options: Product variations
2. Pricing: Variant-specific prices
3. Inventory: Track per-variant stock
4. SKU Management: Unique identifiers
5. Multi-currency: International pricing
