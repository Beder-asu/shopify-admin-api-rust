APPLICATION CREDIT RESOURCE
===========================

DESCRIPTION:
The Application Credit resource allows apps to issue credits to merchants. Credits are deducted from the merchant's upcoming app charges, effectively giving them a discount or refund on their app subscription or usage fees.

PURPOSE:
- Issue refunds or credits to merchants
- Provide promotional discounts
- Compensate for service issues or downtime
- Reward loyal customers

KEY FIELDS:
- id: Unique identifier for the credit
- description: Description of why the credit was issued
- amount: Credit amount (as string, e.g., "10.00")
- test: Whether this is a test credit

AVAILABLE OPERATIONS:
- Find: Get a specific application credit by ID
- List All: Get all application credits
- Create: Issue a new credit to the merchant

USE CASES:
1. Refunds: Issue partial refunds for service issues
2. Promotional Credits: Give new merchants trial credits
3. Loyalty Rewards: Reward long-term customers
4. Service Compensation: Compensate for downtime or bugs
5. Migration Incentives: Offer credits to switch from competitors

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Credits are applied to the merchant's next invoice
- Credits cannot exceed the app's total billing amount
- Once created, credits cannot be deleted or modified
- Test credits are not charged/credited in live transactions
