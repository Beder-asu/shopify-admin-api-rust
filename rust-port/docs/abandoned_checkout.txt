ABANDONED CHECKOUT RESOURCE
===========================

DESCRIPTION:
The Abandoned Checkout resource represents a checkout session that a customer started but did not complete. When a customer adds items to their cart and begins the checkout process but leaves before completing the purchase, Shopify stores this information as an abandoned checkout.

PURPOSE:
- Track incomplete checkout sessions for customer recovery campaigns
- Analyze checkout abandonment patterns
- Enable abandoned cart email notifications
- Recover potentially lost sales through targeted follow-ups

KEY FIELDS:
- id: Unique identifier for the abandoned checkout
- token: Unique token for the checkout session
- cart_token: Token identifying the associated cart
- email: Customer's email address
- gateway: Payment gateway that was selected
- buyer_accepts_marketing: Whether customer opted into marketing
- created_at: When the checkout was created
- updated_at: When the checkout was last updated
- landing_site: URL where the customer landed
- referring_site: URL that referred the customer
- subtotal_price: Subtotal before shipping and taxes
- total_price: Total amount including all charges
- total_tax: Total tax amount
- currency: Currency code (e.g., USD)
- line_items: Array of products in the checkout
- billing_address: Customer's billing address
- shipping_address: Customer's shipping address
- customer: Associated customer information
- abandoned_checkout_url: URL to recover the checkout

AVAILABLE OPERATIONS:
- List all abandoned checkouts (read-only)
- Count abandoned checkouts

USE CASES:
1. Abandoned Cart Recovery Emails: Send automated emails to customers who abandoned their carts
2. Analytics: Track checkout abandonment rates and identify friction points
3. Retargeting: Use abandoned checkout data for remarketing campaigns
4. Customer Service: Help customers complete their purchases
