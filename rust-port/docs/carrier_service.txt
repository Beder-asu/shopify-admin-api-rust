CARRIER SERVICE RESOURCE
========================

DESCRIPTION:
The Carrier Service resource represents a custom shipping carrier integration. It allows apps to provide real-time shipping rates by registering a callback URL that Shopify will query during checkout to get available shipping options.

PURPOSE:
- Integrate custom shipping providers
- Provide real-time shipping rate calculations
- Offer specialized shipping options
- Enable location-based shipping rates

KEY FIELDS:
- id: Unique identifier for the carrier service
- name: Display name shown to merchants and customers
- active: Whether the carrier service is enabled
- service_discovery: Whether to show in carrier service discovery
- carrier_service_type: "api" for callback-based or "legacy" for older integrations
- callback_url: URL Shopify calls to get shipping rates
- format: Response format ("json" or "xml")
- admin_graphql_api_id: GraphQL API identifier

AVAILABLE OPERATIONS:
- Find: Get a specific carrier service by ID
- List All: Get all carrier services for the shop
- Create: Register a new carrier service
- Update: Modify an existing carrier service
- Delete: Remove a carrier service

CALLBACK REQUEST:
When checkout needs rates, Shopify POSTs to your callback_url with:
- Origin address (fulfillment location)
- Destination address
- Items (products, quantities, weights)
- Currency

CALLBACK RESPONSE:
Your service returns available shipping rates including:
- Service name
- Description
- Price
- Estimated delivery time

USE CASES:
1. Custom Carriers: Integrate regional or specialized carriers
2. Dynamic Pricing: Calculate rates based on real-time factors
3. Multi-carrier Support: Aggregate rates from multiple services
4. Location-based Rates: Offer rates based on delivery zones
5. Special Handling: Provide rates for oversized or fragile items
