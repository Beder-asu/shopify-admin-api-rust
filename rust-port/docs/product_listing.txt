PRODUCT LISTING RESOURCE
========================

DESCRIPTION:
The Product Listing resource represents products that have been published to your app's sales channel. It provides product data specifically formatted for external sales channels and marketplaces.

PURPOSE:
- Access channel-published products
- Enable external sales channels
- Sync products to marketplaces
- Support headless commerce

KEY FIELDS:
- product_id: The product's ID
- title: Product name
- body_html: Description HTML
- vendor: Product vendor
- product_type: Category
- handle: URL slug
- published_at: Channel publish date
- created_at / updated_at: Timestamps
- variants: Published variants
- images: Product images
- options: Variant options

LISTING-SPECIFIC:
- Only includes published products
- Optimized for channel consumption
- May differ from admin product data

AVAILABLE OPERATIONS:
- Find: Get a specific product listing
- List All: Get all product listings
- Count: Count product listings
- Product IDs: Get all published product IDs

PUBLISHING WORKFLOW:
1. Product created in Shopify
2. Merchant publishes to your channel
3. Product appears in listings
4. Fetch via Product Listing API
5. Display in your channel

USE CASES:
1. Sales Channels: Build selling platforms
2. Marketplaces: Sync to external markets
3. Mobile Apps: Get published products
4. Headless Stores: Custom storefronts
5. Product Feeds: Generate product feeds
