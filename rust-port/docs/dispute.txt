DISPUTE RESOURCE
================

DESCRIPTION:
The Dispute resource represents a payment dispute (chargeback) in Shopify Payments. Disputes occur when a customer questions a charge with their bank, and the merchant must provide evidence to resolve the dispute.

PURPOSE:
- View payment disputes
- Track dispute status
- Manage chargeback responses
- Monitor financial risk

KEY FIELDS:
- id: Unique identifier for the dispute
- order_id: Associated order ID
- type: Dispute type (chargeback, inquiry)
- currency: Currency of the disputed amount
- amount: Disputed amount
- reason: Reason for the dispute
- network_reason_code: Card network's reason code
- status: Current dispute status
- evidence_due_by: Deadline to submit evidence
- evidence_sent_on: When evidence was submitted
- finalized_on: When dispute was resolved
- initiated_at: When dispute was initiated

AVAILABLE OPERATIONS:
- Find: Get a specific dispute
- List All: Get all disputes with filters

DISPUTE STATUSES:
- needs_response: Awaiting merchant evidence
- under_review: Bank reviewing evidence
- charge_refunded: Resolved in customer's favor
- accepted: Merchant accepted the dispute
- won: Resolved in merchant's favor
- lost: Customer won the dispute

DISPUTE REASONS:
- bank_not_process: Bank processing error
- credit_not_processed: Expected credit not received
- customer_initiated: Customer disputes charge
- duplicate: Duplicate charge
- fraudulent: Unauthorized transaction
- general: Unspecified reason
- incorrect_account_details: Wrong account
- insufficient_funds: Insufficient funds
- product_not_received: Item not delivered
- product_unacceptable: Product quality issue
- subscription_cancelled: Unexpected charge
- unrecognized: Customer doesn't recognize charge

USE CASES:
1. Dispute Management: Track and respond to chargebacks
2. Risk Analysis: Identify dispute patterns
3. Evidence Submission: Prepare dispute responses
4. Financial Reporting: Monitor chargeback costs
