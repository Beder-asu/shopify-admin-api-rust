PRODUCT RESOURCE
================

DESCRIPTION:
The Product resource represents items for sale in a Shopify store. It's a core resource containing product information, variants, images, and metadata needed to display and sell merchandise.

PURPOSE:
- Manage product catalog
- Define items for sale
- Organize product information
- Support product variants

KEY FIELDS:
- id: Unique identifier
- title: Product name
- body_html: Description HTML
- vendor: Product vendor/brand
- product_type: Product category
- handle: URL-friendly slug
- status: active, draft, archived
- published_at: When published
- published_scope: web, global
- template_suffix: Custom template
- tags: Comma-separated tags
- variants: Product variants array
- options: Variant options (size, color)
- images: Product images
- image: Primary image
- created_at / updated_at: Timestamps
- admin_graphql_api_id: GraphQL identifier

VARIANT FIELDS (nested):
- id, product_id, title
- price, compare_at_price
- sku, barcode
- inventory_quantity
- weight, weight_unit
- option1, option2, option3
- inventory_item_id
- fulfillment_service
- requires_shipping, taxable

PRODUCT STATUSES:
- active: Visible for sale
- draft: Work in progress
- archived: No longer for sale

AVAILABLE OPERATIONS:
- Find: Get a specific product
- List All: Get products with filters
- Count: Count products
- Create: Create a new product
- Update: Modify product details
- Delete: Remove a product

USE CASES:
1. Product Catalog: Manage merchandise
2. Inventory Sync: Update from external systems
3. Bulk Operations: Mass product updates
4. Product Import: Create products automatically
5. E-commerce Integration: Connect sales channels
