POLICY RESOURCE
===============

DESCRIPTION:
The Policy resource represents the legal policies for a shop, including refund policy, privacy policy, terms of service, shipping policy, and other legal documents.

PURPOSE:
- Access store policies
- Display legal information
- Ensure compliance
- Manage policy content

KEY FIELDS:
- title: Policy name
- body: HTML policy content
- url: URL where policy is displayed
- handle: URL-friendly identifier
- created_at / updated_at: Timestamps

POLICY TYPES (by handle):
- refund-policy: Return and refund terms
- privacy-policy: Data handling practices
- terms-of-service: Purchase terms
- shipping-policy: Shipping information
- legal-notice: Legal disclaimers
- contact-information: Contact details
- subscription-policy: Subscription terms

AVAILABLE OPERATIONS:
- List All: Get all policies for the shop

READ-ONLY RESOURCE:
- Policies set in Shopify admin
- Settings > Policies
- API provides read access only

POLICY URLS:
- /policies/refund-policy
- /policies/privacy-policy
- /policies/terms-of-service
- /policies/shipping-policy

USE CASES:
1. Footer Links: Display policy links
2. Checkout: Show terms before purchase
3. Legal Compliance: Ensure policy presence
4. Custom Storefronts: Fetch policy content
5. Mobile Apps: Display policies in app
