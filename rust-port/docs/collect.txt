COLLECT RESOURCE
================

DESCRIPTION:
The Collect resource represents the relationship between a product and a custom collection. A collect is essentially a join record that links a product to a collection, allowing products to appear in multiple collections.

PURPOSE:
- Add products to custom collections
- Manage collection memberships
- Control product ordering within collections
- Remove products from collections

KEY FIELDS:
- id: Unique identifier for the collect
- collection_id: ID of the collection
- product_id: ID of the product
- created_at: When the relationship was created
- updated_at: When the relationship was modified
- position: Product's position in the collection
- sort_value: Value used for sorting

AVAILABLE OPERATIONS:
- Find: Get a specific collect by ID
- List All: Get all collects (filterable by collection or product)
- Count: Count collects
- Create: Add a product to a collection
- Delete: Remove a product from a collection

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Only used with Custom Collections, not Smart Collections
- Smart Collections use rules to automatically include products
- A product can belong to multiple collections
- Position determines display order in the collection

USE CASES:
1. Collection Management: Organize products into collections
2. Product Categorization: Assign products to categories
3. Featured Products: Add/remove from featured collections
4. Seasonal Collections: Manage holiday or seasonal groupings
5. Bulk Operations: Mass-add products to collections
